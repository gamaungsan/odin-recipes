Pound Cake

Description

Elegant and simple, this pound cake is the little black dress of dessert.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 1 cup or 2 sticks of unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon of baking powder
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cup of milk - optional

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven at 350 degree Fahrenheit
  2. Mix sugar and butter for about 8 min at medium setting
  3. Add each egg one at a time to the mixture
  4. Add vanilla extract, salt, baking powder, and milk
  5. Slowly add the flour
  6. Mix til the batter has a good consistency
  7. Bake it for about an hour
